Explore. Innovate. Perform.

AsRC challenges teams to design rovers that combine mechanical innovation, intelligent software, robust electronics, and effective operational strategies into a single, mission-ready system.

Autonomous Navigation

Navigate through a simulated planetary terrain while avoiding obstacles, planning efficient routes, and operating with minimal human intervention. Evaluates localization, path planning, and mobility.

Equipment Servicing

Perform precision manipulation tasks using robotic arms and end-effectors to interact with mission equipment. Demonstrates accurate control, dexterity, and reliable operation.

Science Exploration

Collect, transport, and analyze simulated planetary samples to investigate the environment. Assesses sampling techniques, onboard science systems, and data interpretation.

Settlement Operations

Support sustainable extraterrestrial habitat development by completing construction and infrastructure tasks to assist future human missions.

Mission Management

Operate the rover through a professional mission control workflow while demonstrating effective planning, communication, situational awareness, and decision-making. Evaluates the team's ability to monitor health and coordinate efficiently under time constraints.
